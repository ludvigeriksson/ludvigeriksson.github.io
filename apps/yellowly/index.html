Yellowly

A minimalistic game exclusive to tvOS

Use the touch pad to navigate the player and dodge the enemy shapes. Collect as many white dots as possible before being hit.

Download

Yellowly is available for free in the Apple TV App Store. It is listed as 'TV Game - Yellowly. Dodge & Escape!' from Top TV Apps.
