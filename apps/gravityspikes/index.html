Gravity Spikes

A minimalistic side scrolling game for iOS and tvOS built with Sprite Kit

Tap to flip the gravity to make the ball fall in the opposite direction. Time these flips to avoid the colored spikes. Black platforms are your friends, use them to navigate your way through the obstacles. Try to survive for as long as you can.
