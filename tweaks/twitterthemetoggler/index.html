TwitterThemeToggler

Quickly toggle night mode in the official Twitter app

Twitter's night mode is beautiful, but it requires four taps to activate from your timeline. With TwitterThemeToggler, simply long press the navigation bar and watch the transition happen.

Download

TwitterThemeToggler is available for free from BigBoss.

  • Open in Cydia
  • See Cydia package

Compatibility

TwitterThemeToggler was developed for Twitter 6.68.1. If an update to the app breaks the tweak, please let me know (contact info below).

Latest tested working version: 6.71.
