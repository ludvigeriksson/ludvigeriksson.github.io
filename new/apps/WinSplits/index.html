WinSplits for iPhone

The official WinSplits iPhone client

Automatically lists the latest events

On the app's startup page the latest week's events are automatically listed for easy access.

A simple and clean UI that makes it easy to analyze your split times

One control is shown at a time, swipe to reveal the next. Regardless of how far you scroll, you will always know which control and which runner the times belong to.

See how far behind you are on each leg

With a single press you can see how far behind the fastest runner you are, both on the leg and in total.

See how much you have missed

If you see a split time in bold it means time loss have occurred. Click twice to show the time loss.

Save classes offline

When you have looked at a class it is automatically saved to your phone. The events that have classes saved are automatically listed on the app's startup page under downloaded events. You can delete these any time, either one event at a time, or all at once.

Adjusts text size automatically

WinSplits for iPhone takes advantage of Dynamic Type and matches your preferred reading size, which is set under Settings > General > Text size.

Download

WinSplits for iPhone is a free download on the App Store.

About WinSplits

WinSplits is a software system created for presentation and analysis of split times from orienteering competitions. It is developed by Mats Troeng.
