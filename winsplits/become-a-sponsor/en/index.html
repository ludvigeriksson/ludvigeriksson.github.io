Users of The WinSplits app are all within our narrow orienteering section of the market. Take the chance to appear right at their fingertips.

How it works

Every sponsored message consists of two images so they will look good under all circumstances.

  • Standard: 1080 x 608 pixles (16:9 format)
  • Wide: 1920 x 540 pixels (32:9 format)

A close button will appear in the upper right corner of all images to give the user a consistent experience. You can choose to have a black or white close button depending on what looks best on your image. Avoid putting text or other detailed information here.

Messages are shown in three different views of the app; in the starting view with competitions, in the view with classes and in the result view. Only one message will be displayed on any given page. If multiple messages are available one will be picked at random.

Sponsors are displayed in The WinSplits App since version 2.0. In November 2017 this version reached 4000 downloads.
